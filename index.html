1/31/2022 Meeting

Change 4 Change Project

Today we went over our world water crisis project.


(1) We are going to be conducting our first change 4 change project.

(2) This project is partnering with the Blue Missions group.

(3) During the month of March, Riviera Prep students will engage in a fun competition to raise money. Each grade will get their own jug.
You can get an hour after school next monday for helping to decorate the jugs.
Later on, there will be other possible community service opportunities including:

* Presenting the project during lunch
* Presenting the project for the news
* Counting the pennies

1 cent = 1 point
1 dollar = -100 points
5 dollars = -500 points

Why the negative points?

Participants can donate dollars to opposing grades, negating their points.

There is a planned (but yet to be disclosed!) prize for the winner grade.

IMPORTANT DATES

  • 2/18 Update 1 @ lunches
